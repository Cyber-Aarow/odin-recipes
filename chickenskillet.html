30-Minute Italian Chicken Skillet

Description

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Button mushrooms
  • Chicken breasts
  • Red pepper
  • Red onion
  • Asparagus
  • Zucchini
  • Italian seasoning
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Basil
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Saute the mushrooms and garlic.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned.
  3. Add the Italian seasoning, balsamic vinegar, and basil.
  4. Mix in the veggies and cook until tender.
  5. Stir in the parmesan cheese.
  6. Serve with some rice or quinoa!
