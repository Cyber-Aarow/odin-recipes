Italian Stuffed Zucchini Boats

Description

Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Olive oil
  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Celery Stalks
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning blend of basil, oregano, rosemary, and thyme
  • Salt and pepper
  • Extra-lean ground beaf or chicken
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Chopped basil
  • Cherry tomatoes

Steps

  1. Scoop out the zucchinis.
  2. Saute the veggies.
  3. Add in the meat.
  4. Mix in the rest of the ingredients.
  5. Scoop the mixture in to the zucchini boats.
  6. Top with cheese.
  7. Bake for 20 minutes.
  8. Garnish and serve!
